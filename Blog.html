Eduvision Career Counseling App

Advantages

Eduvision app provides students and parents the timely information and guidance with easy-to-use features.
It covers the latest education news, latest admission updates, scholarship updates, careers introduction, education updates, and career counseling videos.

Disadvantages

Does not have the option to connect with a counsellor on the app.

RightPath Career Counselling App

Advantages

Offers connecting to counsellors via taking an appointment.

Disadvantages

Does not have the option for any news related to counselling and it also doesn't have the option for counsellors to register themselves.
