About Us

Welcome to our career counselling app! Our goal is to help students and professionals alike take the next step in their career journey.

For students, our app offers a platform to connect with experienced counsellors who can guide you in making informed decisions about your future. You can also stay up-to-date on the latest news and opportunities in your field of interest.

For qualified counsellors, our app is a great way to find job opportunities and connect with potential clients. You can create a profile and make yourself available to those seeking guidance.

Overall, our app aims to be a one-stop-shop for career counselling and advice. We hope it helps you achieve your goals and make your dreams a reality.
